Who are we

We Are

University of Science Accommodation - Karabuk, the first accommodation for Yemeni students in Turkey, working under license number (178158700015) issued by the Turkish Ministry of Youth and Sports On 02/15/2021 AD, it is concerned with providing a safe and supportive residential environment for Yemeni and international students. Students at Karabuk University, to enable them to complete their educational careers, Providing them with knowledge and providing them with various life and job skills.

Our message

We seek to provide a safe and distinguished accommodation environment for Yemeni and international students. Students at Karabuk University with the aim of nurturing and qualifying them with various life skills and knowledge, and preparing them to build the future through various training activities and programs.

Our vision

The best model in caring for the student and qualifying him with the necessary knowledge and skills

Our values

Ambition - Excellence - Creativity

Accommodation Objectives

  • Providing a suitable residential environment for the care of students and the development of their knowledge and skills..
  • Enhancing students’ academic excellence, discovering their talents, developing their abilities, and adopting their creativity
  • Promoting virtuous values, consolidating lofty principles, and preserving the Islamic identity among students
  • Reducing the financial burdens of students who are stranded and whose livelihood is narrow

Accommodation features

  • The building is officially licensed and is characterized by high specifications and high-end equipment.
  • The building is furnished and equipped with all the appropriate equipment and facilities, according to the legal specifications for Accommodations
  • Complete accommodation services with all necessary facilities (swimming pool - garden - library - gym - training hall - prayer room)
